About

SSBMAI is a reinforcement learning bot for the game Super Smash Brothers on the Nintendo Gamecube. It runs atop the Dolphin Emulator, providing a way for Melee afficianatos to improve their craft. What really sets our bot apart from the others is that it learns as it plays, adapting to a myriad of characters, stages, and scenarios. Our bot is also ablee to perform highly technical moves, preparing those who use it to hone their skills a more realistic, human-like training partner.
